The Beauty Of Nature
Nature is one of the most important part of God's creation. Vegetables are planted, so that we can eat it to live. It gives us nutrients that helps our body to become fit and well. Trees also has a major role in our daily lives, it helps us breath clean air. Trees also help in making the soil more compact because, it roots absorbs water if it rains , so that the soil will not erode. Nature is used for decoration because, it is pleasing to the eyes.
Things that could harm the environment
We know what are the importance of nature in our lives yet, we still destroy them. Even if we humans benefits the most in nature, we were also the ones that destroys them the most. Some people always throw plastics everywhere without even knowing what the consequences are. Some people also cut trees so much, so that they can build their house, to have bigger land and even make other products. Smoking can also harm the environment and health condition of the human body.
Effects
The effects of ruining nature is quite destructive and dangerous. The effects of cutting trees is soil erosion, because there are no roots that holds the soil together. Smoking can cause air pollution that leads other people to have health problems. It can also lead to climate change which causes strong stroms that can destroy houses.
Maintaining Nature
We need to maintain nature so that we can prevent calamities and disasters. After we cut trees we should plant some replacement in order to maintain the number of trees. If you have plastic garbages or other garbage that can be use again, we can recycle it and make it into something new.
